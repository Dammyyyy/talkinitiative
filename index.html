The recovery guide

The recovery guide

A 30 day guide to kickstart your healing journey

The Recovery Guide is a mental health resource that aims to ease traumatic responses, reactions and emotions due to sexual abuse.
The Recovery Guide practically addresses the deepest fears and hardest questions on the mind of every sexual abuse survivor, in a 30-day journey of confronting pain, unloading oppressive emotional burdens and embracing healing. Each day is designed to address a salient subject in the following sections; Acknowledgments, Actions and Affirmations.
